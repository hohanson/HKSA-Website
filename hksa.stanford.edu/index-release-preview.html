Hong Kong Student Association at Stanford.

A home away from home for Hong Kong students, friends, and anyone who enjoys our culture.

Get Involved
Who We Are

A Hong Kong community at Stanford.

We are a group of undergraduate and graduate students dedicated to preserving and promoting Hong Kong culture at Stanford, and connecting students passionate about the culture.

What We Do

Events throughout the year.

From food events and mahjong nights to karaoke and movie screenings, we bring Hong Kong culture to life on campus. There’s always something on.

Annual

New Member Welcome

Kick off the year by meeting fellow HKSA members, learning about what we do, and finding your community at Stanford.

Social

Open Quarter Rice

A shared meal at the start of each quarter to welcome new faces, reconnect with old friends, and begin the term together as a community.

Food

Food & Gather

Casual on-campus hangouts with Hong Kong food, mahjong, karaoke, and plenty of time to catch up with friends.

Social

Mahjong Night

More than just a game, mahjong has long been a staple of social life in Hong Kong. Join us for an evening of strategy, laughter, and friendly competition.

Social

Karaoke Night

From Cantopop classics to the latest hits, gather for a night of music, laughter, and singing with friends.

Social

Movie Night

Enjoy Cantonese films with fellow members and discover the stories, humor, and nostalgia that resonate with generations of Hongkongers.

Cultural

Winter Solstice

Celebrated widely in Hong Kong, the Winter Solstice is a time for reunion and togetherness. Join us as we mark the occasion with food and community.

Cultural

Chinese New Year

Celebrate the start of the new year with fai chun calligraphy, festive treats, and traditional customs as we share one of Hong Kong’s most cherished celebrations.

Annual

Chinese New Year Hotpot

Gather around the hotpot table to celebrate the beginning of a new year with students and alumni, sharing good food and good company.

Food

Dim Sum Run

Explore some of the Bay Area’s best dim sum restaurants and experience one of Hong Kong’s most beloved dining traditions.

Social

Hongkongers in the Bay Area

Connect with Hong Kong professionals and alumni across a range of industries, fostering mentorship and cultural connections across generations.

Social

Berkeley Mixer

Strengthen ties with fellow Hong Kong students across the Bay Area and celebrate our shared culture.

Annual

Spring Retreat

An off-campus getaway to relax, explore, and create lasting memories as we celebrate another year together.

Annual

Year-End Celebration

Close out the academic year with hotpot, karaoke, and a chance to celebrate our graduating members and reconnect with alumni.
